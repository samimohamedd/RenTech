Privacy Policy


Your privacy matters to us. Learn how we protect and handle your information with complete transparency.

Last updated: January 2025


Welcome to Renturn. We care about your privacy and want you to feel safe while using our services. This Privacy Policy explains clearly what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we keep it safe. Using Renturn means you agree to this policy, so take a moment to read it and understand how we protect your information.

Information We Collect

We collect information to provide you with the best possible rental experience. Here’s what we might collect:

Personal Info

  • Your name, email, phone number for bookings, National ID
  • Account details if you create a Renturn account

Payment Info

  • Credit card or other payment details processed securely
  • Transaction history related to rentals or purchases

Location Info

  • Helps us deliver or pick up rentals efficiently.

Usage Info

  • Pages visited, items booked, actions on the website
  • Device/browser info to improve website performance

Communication Info

  • Messages, emails, or support requests sent to us.

How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide and manage Renturn services smoothly
  • Confirm bookings, send reminders, communicate updates
  • Improve website/services based on usage patterns
  • Send promotional offers only if you opted in

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your data.
  • May share info with **trusted partners** like payment processors or delivery services
  • Disclose info if **legally required** or to protect Renturn and users

Keeping Your Data Safe

Encryption and secure systems protect sensitive info.

Only authorized team members can access personal info.

Security practices are regularly updated.

Your Rights

You are in control:

  • Request a copy of your info
  • Delete data (if legally allowed)
  • Correct/update info
  • Opt-out of marketing emails anytime

Cookies and Tracking

Essential cookies

Required for website functionality.

Preference cookies

Remember your settings.

Performance cookies

Help analyze website usage.

Marketing cookies

Only if opted-in.

Third-Party Links

Not responsible for privacy practices of linked sites. Check their policies before sharing info.

Policy Updates

Policy may be updated occasionally for **new services or security improvements**. Check back periodically.
